# Aetheron AI Technologies Pvt Ltd - Website PRD

## Original Problem Statement
Build a premium, ultra-sleek, black and graphite gray website for Aetheron AI Technologies Pvt Ltd, an advanced AI and machine intelligence company. The design must feel like a funded deep-tech startup — cinematic, minimal, futuristic, and powerful.

## User Personas
- **Investors**: Looking for next-gen AI/industrial tech investments
- **Enterprise Clients**: Industrial companies seeking AI automation solutions
- **Industrial Partners**: Manufacturing and production facility operators
- **Deep-tech Enthusiasts**: Technology professionals in AI/ML space

## Core Requirements (Static)
- Black (#000000) + Graphite Gray (#111111) theme
- Metallic silver gradient accents
- Elite, industrial, precise, dominant tone
- Cinematic, minimal, futuristic design
- Interactive 3D Spline background
- Custom cursor experience
- Scroll-triggered animations

## What's Been Implemented (Feb 2025)
- ✅ Loading screen with animated progress bar (0-100%)
- ✅ Custom cursor with silver ring, magnetic effects, hover states
- ✅ Hero section with Spline 3D iframe background
- ✅ Animated typography with letter-by-letter reveal
- ✅ About section with split layout, stats, and image
- ✅ AetherBuilt project showcase with glass panels
- ✅ Animated counter numbers (machines online, CPU efficiency, etc.)
- ✅ Features section with 6 icon cards and hover effects
- ✅ Immersive parallax section with floating particles
- ✅ Minimal footer with contact links
- ✅ Framer Motion animations throughout
- ✅ Responsive design (desktop/mobile)

## Tech Stack
- **Frontend**: React 19, Tailwind CSS, Framer Motion
- **3D**: Spline (iframe embed)
- **Fonts**: Manrope, Inter, JetBrains Mono
- **Icons**: Lucide React
